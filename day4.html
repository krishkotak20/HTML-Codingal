Introduction to Football

Football is a globally popular sport played between two teams. It is known for its intensity, teamwork, and strategic play.


Key Terms

Goal
The act of scoring by getting the ball into the opposing team's net.
Offside
A rule violation when a player is closer to the opponent's goal than the ball and second-last defender.
Penalty
A free shot at goal awarded after a serious foul inside the penalty area.

Basic Rules

  1. Each team has eleven players on the field.
  2. The match lasts for ninety minutes, split into two halves.
  3. Only the goalkeeper can use hands within the penalty box.
  4. Fouls result in free kicks or penalties depending on severity and location.

Essential Equipment

  • Football boots
  • Shin guards
  • Jersey and shorts
  • Goalkeeper gloves

Conclusion: Football combines physical skill, mental focus, and tactical awareness. Whether played casually or professionally, it remains one of the most engaging sports worldwide.
