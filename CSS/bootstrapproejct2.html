Football

is a globally popular sport played between two teams of eleven players on a rectangular field with a goal at each end. The game is played primarily with the feet, and players advance the ball by dribbling, passing, or shooting. The objective is to score goals by getting the ball into the opponent’s net, while the defending team tries to block attacks and
prevent goals
. The field is typically about 
100–110 meters long
and 64–75 meters wide, with markings that define areas such as the penalty box and center circle. Passing, tackling, and positioning are key elements of play.
