Cambridge International School,Abu Dhabi CIA

Introduction

GEMS Cambridge International School Abu Dhabi (CIA) in Baniyas is a private British curriculum school offering education from Foundation Stage to Year 13. It is part of the GEMS Education network and follows the National Curriculum for England, preparing students for IGCSEs, A Levels, and BTEC qualifications. The school is located in Bawabat Al Sharq, Baniyas East, and serves a diverse student body of over 3400 learners. It emphasizes academic excellence, innovation, and global citizenship. Students consistently perform above UK national averages and gain admission to universities in more than 25 countries. Facilities include modern classrooms, science and ICT labs, a swimming pool, indoor sports areas, and dedicated spaces for performing arts and vocational training. The school also offers a wide range of extracurricular activities including sports, music, drama, and Model United Nations. The leadership team, headed by Principal Hazel Govender(who is now leaving), focuses on personalized learning and holistic development. The school promotes values such as respect, responsibility, and resilience, aiming to nurture confident and capable global citizens.

CIA School Baniyas integrates STEM across all key stages through hands-on learning, interdisciplinary projects, and digital innovation. Students engage with robotics, coding, and scientific inquiry from early years onward, supported by modern labs and tech-rich classrooms. The curriculum emphasizes real-world problem solving and critical thinking, preparing learners for global challenges and future careers in science, engineering, and technology.

Our Location

CIA School Baniyasis located in Bawabat Al Sharq, a modern residential and commercial district in Baniyas East, Abu Dhabi. The campus sits near key community hubs, offering easy access for families across the region.
